🌏 Today's Cultural Fact

🇯🇵 Japan

In Japan, it's customary to remove your shoes when entering someone's home. This tradition dates back to the Edo period and helps keep living spaces clean. When visiting a Japanese home, look for a small step down at the entrance called a genkan.

🇧🇷 Brazil
