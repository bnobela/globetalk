About GlobeTalk

GlobeTalk is a platform that connects people from different countries through pen pal letters, helping users learn about other cultures, make lifelong friends, and share experiences globally.

Our mission is to foster meaningful connections and cultural exchange in a safe, friendly, and easy-to-use environment.
